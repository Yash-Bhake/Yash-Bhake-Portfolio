About Me

Bridging AI and Creative Expression

I'm a fourth-year student at IIT Bombay pursuing a Minor Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science under C-MInDS. My research focuses on computational modeling of expressiveness in Hindustani vocal music, combining my passion for music with cutting-edge AI techniques.

With publications in top-tier conferences like ISMIR and ICASSP, I've contributed to the field of Music Information Retrieval through innovative approaches to generative modeling and expressive timing analysis. My work has been recognized with the Undergraduate Research Award and author grants.

Beyond research, I have industry experience at Deutsche Bank where I developed production-ready software solutions, and I'm actively involved in mentoring and teaching roles at IIT Bombay.
