About Me

Bridging AI and Creative Expression

I'm a fourth-year student at IIT Bombay pursuing a Minor Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science. My research focuses on the computational modeling of expressiveness in Hindustani vocal music, combining my passion for music with cutting-edge AI techniques.

With publications in top-tier conferences like ISMIR and ICASSP, I've contributed to the field of Music Information Retrieval through innovative approaches to generative modeling and expressive timing analysis.

Beyond research, I have industry experience at Deutsche Bank where I developed production-ready software solutions. I'm passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems and create meaningful impact.
